Data Wrangling

Before we start transforming and cleaning the data, here's a brief description of some columns:

  • Date: Date of accident, in the format - January 01, 2001
  • Time: Local time, in 24 hr
  • Airline/Op: Airline or operator of the aircraft.
  • Flight #: Flight number assigned by the aircraft operator
  • Route: Complete or partial route flown prior to the accident
  • AC Type: Aircraft type
  • Reg: ICAO registration of the aircraft
  • cn / ln: Construction or serial number / Line or fuselage number
  • Aboard: Total aboard (passengers / crew)
  • Fatalities: Total fatalities aboard (passengers / crew)
  • Ground: Total killed on the ground
  • Summary: Brief description of the accident and cause if known

Exploratory Data Analysis

For an initial analyse we will try to answer the following questions:

  1. How many total fatalities occurred, and how many accidents were recorded?
  2. Has there been any observable trend in fatalities over time, Were there any significant spikes or notable events impacting the number of fatalities?
  3. Which airplane models were involved in the highest number of accidents?
  4. Which manufacturers had the most airplanes involved in accidents?
  5. Among the operators, which ones had the highest number of accidents?
  6. In terms of known routes, which cities had the highest number of accidents originating from?

Let's dive in?

After a quick analysis, we can finally answer the initial questions:

  1. 120398 Total Fatalities in 5043 Accidents
  2. From 1999, the tendency of Fatalities has been decreasing, however, due to the 09/11 attacks, there was a huge spike
  3. "Douglas DC-3" is by far the Airplane involved in the most accidents totalizing 333, second we have the "de Havilland Canada DHC-6 Twin Otter 300" with 83
  4. As for manufacturers "Douglas" aeroplanes were involved in 995 and "Boeing"'s 415
  5. When it comes to operators, "Aeroflot" had 260 accidents and "U.S. Air Force" 141
  6. For the Accidents where routes are known, 65 of the accidents originated from New York and the other 51 from Paris
