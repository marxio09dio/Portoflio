About Me

Born in the 90s in Braga, Portugal.

After finishing high school, I took a detour to do a tour in the army. For more than 6 years, I was a Military Police and served in "Regimento de Lanceiros n2" in Lisbon.

I got the chance to go on a challenging adventure to Kabul, Afghanistan. Quite the experience.

During my time in the army, I instructed fresh MPs and showed them our ways, making sure that important people like ministers and high-ranking officers were safe and sound.

After all these years of being in uniform, I found myself in the driver's seat of an Uber. Oh my, the stories I could tell about the wild encounters and cultural exchanges I had with people from all walks of life!
From the lively conversations about food to those hilarious wrong-turn mishaps, or even that time I almost got kidnapped, my Uber days (or should I say nights? Working from 7 p.m. to 7 a.m. was interesting) were nothing short of an adventure.

I then decided to switch and took on a role as a customer service representative for a major tech company.
After a couple of years, I became a team leader. It was during this time that I discovered my hidden passion for data.
Crunching numbers and analyzing trends became one of the things I enjoy the most about this role, and judging by my team results, it looks like I'm good at it. To you guys: You Are The Best!
